Grace Murray Hopper

    • Nickname: Amazing Grace
    • Born: December 9, 1906 in New York City, New York, U.S.
    • Died: January 1, 1992 (aged 85) in Arlington, Virginia, U.S.
    • Place of burial: Arlington National Cemetery
    • Allegiance: United States of America
    • Service/branch: United States Navy
    • Years of service: 1943-1966, 1967-1971, 1972-1986
    • Rank: Rear admiral (lower half)
    • Awards: Defense Distinguised Service Medal, Legion Merit, Meritorious Service Medal, American Campaign Medal, World War II Victory Meda, National Defense SErvice Medal, Armed Forces Reserve Medal with two HOurglass Devices, Naval Reserve Medal, Presidential Medal of Freedom (posthumous)
